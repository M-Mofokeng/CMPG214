This my reflection on the comminacteYou assignment.
When i first got the assignment i thought it would really be easy, as soon as started i realised ho wrong i was.
The guiding question " If this was the only thing anyone saw about you, what would they think" seemed so simple yet so deep.
When i first saw it i thought systems, i like systems, everything is a system, systems can be brocken, well that's what i would want someon to know about me.
As soon as i got to working it was hard finding a way to convey that under with the constraints that were given to us.
I thought of making it like a character at fisrt and giving myself an alias and make it as if i was expaing my life but in thrid person.
As if my life were a movie and i had just been watching it all along i was trying to explain who the chracter was to someone.
Well ofcourse that's something similar to what i had to do but i had done it all wrong.
I had wanted to make the chracter mysterious and sophisticated isntead of just saying who he is in plain text.
I even used slides for different aspects of his(me) life.
It wasnt till i sent it to for a review and got feedback twice i got to understand what the project was actually all about.
Communicating yourself under constraints, trying to be as expressvive as they allow you.
It was a stressful yet very fun assignment.
I got to learn alot about myself.
